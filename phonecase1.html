Description of Phone Case

A phone case is an accessory that encases a mobile phone to protect it from damage, scratches, and wear. Phone cases come in various materials, including silicone, plastic, leather, and metal, and are designed to fit specific phone models. They can also feature different designs, colors, and functionalities, such as cardholders or kickstands. Using a phone case can help extend the lifespan of a device and maintain its resale value.
